Organic food is often fresher, has fewer pesticides, and contains no GMOs. It can also be more nutritious and have higher antioxidant levels than non-organic food. Organic meat and milk may be richer in certain nutrients, and organic farming helps combat antibiotic resistance. Eating organic food can also improve digestion, energy levels, and the immune system. Organic farming tends to be better for the environment because organic products are sourced from natural products and don't contain pesticides or fertilizers that can harm the environment. Some say that organic food tastes better because of its higher antioxidant levels.


MAJOR BENIFTS OF CONSUMING HEALTHY FOOD ARE:

  • less risk of cancer
  • more environmentally sustainable
  • support ethical farming
  • fresher food
  • more nutrients

ORGANIC FOODS

Organic farming is the production of food without the use of synthetic chemicals or genetically modified components. Organic foods are not necessarily completely chemical free, but the pesticide residues will be considerably lower than those found in produce manufactured with synthetic chemicals.
